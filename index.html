UPCOMING CLASSES, EVENTS & WORKSHOPS

Ritmos Latinos dance studio offers Salsa, Bachata, Merengue and Tango classes. We have something for every student: From beginner, intermediate to advanced, and even for that occasional date night or “want to do something different” night. Change your life, join our dance classes! We have made a difference in so many people's lives.
