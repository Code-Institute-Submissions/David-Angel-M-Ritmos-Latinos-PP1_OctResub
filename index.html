Learn how to dance Latin American rhythms

Ritmos Latinos dance studio offers Salsa, Bachata, Merengue and Tango classes. We have something for every student: From beginner, intermediate to advanced, and even for that occasional date night or “want to do something different” night.

Come and join us for our monthly workshops held in our studio, make sure you write down your name and book in advanced as there's limited spaces only.


Every other last Saturday of the month, after our workshop, we have a night out with the students that want to join us and put in practice what you've learned so far.


About us

We are a young group of qualify dancers from different backgrounds in Latin America that decided to embrace our knowledge in terms of teaching and couching people from all ages that wants to learn how to dance in a fun, relax way and be comfortable in a great atmosphere. We have 4 different genres which you can choose from: Salsa, Bachata, Merengue and Tango and we also have 3 different levels, beginners, intermediate and advanced so you can choose the one that suits you best.
