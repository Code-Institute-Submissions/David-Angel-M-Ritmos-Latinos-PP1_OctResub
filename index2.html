Ritmos Latinos dance studio offers Salsa, Bachata, Merengue and Tango classes. We have something for every student: From beginner, intermediate to advanced, and even for that occasional date night or “want to do something different” night.

Come and join us for our monthly workshops held in our studio, make sure you write down your name and book in advanced as there's limited spaces only. Every other last Saturday of the month, after our workshop, we have a night out with the students that want to join us and put in practice what you've learned so far.
